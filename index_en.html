What is EBEC?

EBEC - European BEST Engineering Competition is a competition designed to encourage creative thinking to solve complex problems.

Categories

Team Design

The goal of the team design category is the students, who are deployed in teams, to find solutions and demonstrate the results in a limited period of time. As a final result, each team presents its device or structure which performs the necessary functions. Teams need to calculate and construct a device or model that meets the specific requirements for a limited period of time. Materials, tools and the necessary instructions are provided on the day of the match by the organizers in charge. The results are evaluated according to predefined rules by a jury.

Case Study

Case study is an intensive analysis that provides a specific solution to a technological or managerial problem. Each team must consider the problem in detail and to offer suggestions that would contribute to his dismissal. This category tests the students' ability to think analytically and their ability to develop a hypothetical solution using the information provided. At the end of the game, each team has its own presentation to the jury.

Што е БЕСТ?

Since 1989 BEST has provided opportunities for communication, cooperation and student exchange across Europe. In 33 countries with a total of 95 Local BEST Groups BEST establishes a student network which is constantly growing, and is also well organized, powerful and innovative. The daily progress is due to around 4800 students who act enthusiastically, while almost 1 million students are covered by influential events and initiatives. BEST gives students the opportunity to expand their knowledge on topics that complement their education by organizing academic courses, engineering competitions, workshops for education, training and cultural exchanges. On the other hand, BEST also expands the labor market through job fairs and career workshops, and also leading an international center for career. Last but not least, BEST has developed its own training system, so our members also have the opportunity to improve their skills and knowledge by participating in trainshops??? for technical skills and "soft skills."

Бест Скопје

Driven by a desire for development of student services and engineering upgrade, as well as improving the student standards, on 8 November 2002 the presidential meeting in Bucharest, Romania, BEST Skopje took the first steps in the big European BEST - family, and received the status of "baby member". Along with the status, BEST Skopje got a liability to organize at least one BEST Course each year which would be attended by around 30 students from all over Europe. BEST Skopje has a responsibility to cover all costs associated with their stay in Macedonia. BEST Skopje has managed to successfully fulfill this responsibility for thirteen years, starting with the successful implementation of the first International Academic Course in July 2003. As a result of their effort and success, on 4 November 2003, BEST Skopje has gained the status of "full member "and stepped equally with all local BEST groups from across Europe. Since 2005, BEST Skopje has begun with the traditional organization of the Fair Employment and Career - Job Fair. Since 2010 BEST Skopje has begun to organize another event - Local engineering competition, which aims to give young and innovative engineers to highlight their knowledge and creativity. The success of BEST Skopje is even greater, given the fact that BEST Skopje is the only student organization of its kind which offers all students, not just their members, further education in universities across Europe.
